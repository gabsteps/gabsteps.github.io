<h1>What Is This Website?</h1>

In short, this is a portfolio website and personal blog currently under development where I share my personal projects and research, mainly focusing on cybersecurity-related content.

<h1>Current State</h1>

The basic CSS and HTML structure is finished, and the default layouts for each type of content have been created to make maintenance easier.

<h1>Future objectives</h1>

The main idea is to maintain a clean and minimalist visual design. From now on, I will mainly focus on fine-tuning the visuals based on personal observations or suggestions.

Soon I will start showcasing some personal projects on the “My Projects” page. The Articles page will receive new posts about my personal research and studies. Over time, both pages will gradually be populated with more content.

This website will serve as a record of my learning process and technical exploration in cybersecurity.
