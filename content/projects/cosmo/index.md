# COSMO

COSMO is an experimental social robotics and local-first AI project designed to explore human-computer interaction, embodied assistant behavior, modular automation, and autonomous system architecture.

The project focuses on building a structured robotic assistant platform with components for speech recognition, text-to-speech, wake word handling, memory, vision experiments, web interfaces, logging, automation, and technical documentation.

Rather than being a simple voice assistant, COSMO is designed as a social robot prototype: a system that combines interaction, personality, perception, and local services into a cohesive experimental platform.

Visit the dedicated COSMO website/wiki and source code.
